MY FAVOURITE ANIME

SHIN-CHAN クレヨンしんちゃん

Shin chan is 5 year old boy He is a very troublesome kid
Shin-chan" is the nickname of the main character in the manga and anime series Crayon
The character's full name is Shinnosuke Nohara (野原しんのすけ). Crayon Shin-chan first appeared in 1990 in the Japanese magazine Weekly Manga
The series was written and illustrated by Yoshito Usui. The Nohara family's antics were popular in Japan, but were a cultural phenomenon in Spain.
